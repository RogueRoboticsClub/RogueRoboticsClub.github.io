Rogue Robotics

an underwater robotics club based in Holmdel, NJ

What We Do

We're a team of eleven high school students who like robots and build underwater ROVs to compete in the MATE ROV competition. Our mission is to develop underwater vehicles that address challenging marine issues. Our team members also give back to the community by hosting nonprofit coding workshops and attending technology conferences to exhibit and talk about robotic technology.
